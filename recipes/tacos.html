The Best Taco Recipe Ever

Description

This is the best taco recipe that you will find on the internet. This is going to be perfectly seasoned and paired with delicious rice and margarita. These tacos will guarentee your family will thank you for visiting this site.

Ingredents

  • 1tsp. salt
  • 1tsp. pepper
  • 2 limes
  • 3tbsp. taco seasoning
  • 6 flour tortillas
  • 1 bundle of cilantro
  • Olive oil
  • 1.5oz repo tequila
  • .5oz simple syrup
  • .75oz triple sec
  • .5oz lime juice
  • 1lb. carne asada

Cooking Instructions

  1. Preheat a skillet over med-high heat and coat pan with olive oil.
  2. Season the carne asada with the taco seasoning.
  3. Once pan is hot, add carne asada and cook to desired temp.
  4. Add rice 1 cup of dry rice to hot pot and add 2 cups of water. Cook on medium heat until water is desolved.
  5. Fry flour tortillas until warm and lightly browned
  6. Once carne asada is cooked, cut into chunks and add to the tortillas. Top with avocado, salsa and cilantro.
  7. Once rice is cooked, remove from heat and mix with cilantro and the juice of two limes.
  8. Plate rice with the completed tacos
  9. Mix the tequila, simple syrup, triple sec and lime juice in a shaker cup and shake for 30 sec.
  10. Pour into a glass over ice.
  11. ENJOY!
